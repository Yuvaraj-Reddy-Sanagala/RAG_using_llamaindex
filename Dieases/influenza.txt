Name: Influenza (Flu)
Symptoms: Fever, chills, body aches, sore throat, cough.
Causes: Infection by influenza virus spread through droplets.
Prevention: Annual flu vaccine, hand hygiene.
Treatment: Antiviral drugs, rest, fluids.
Diagnosis: Rapid flu test, clinical symptoms.
Complications: Pneumonia, hospitalization, death in severe cases.
Contagious: Yes
Affected Population: All ages, especially elderly and immunocompromised.
Type: Acute
