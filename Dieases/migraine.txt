Name: Migraine
Symptoms: Severe headache (often one-sided), nausea, sensitivity to light and sound, visual auras.
Causes: Stress, hormonal changes, certain foods, lack of sleep, genetics.
Prevention: Avoiding triggers, stress management, regular sleep patterns.
Treatment: Pain relievers, triptans, anti-nausea drugs, preventive medications.
Diagnosis: Clinical evaluation, patient history.
Complications: Chronic migraine, medication overuse headache.
Contagious: No
Affected Population: Teens and adults, especially women.
Type: Chronic
