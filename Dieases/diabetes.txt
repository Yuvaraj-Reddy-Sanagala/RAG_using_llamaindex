Name: Diabetes (Type 2)
Symptoms: Frequent urination, excessive thirst, fatigue, blurred vision, slow wound healing.
Causes: Obesity, sedentary lifestyle, high sugar diet, genetics, age over 45.
Prevention: Healthy diet, regular exercise, weight control, routine screening.
Treatment: Metformin, insulin (if needed), lifestyle changes.
Diagnosis: Blood glucose tests, HbA1c test.
Complications: Heart disease, kidney failure, blindness, nerve damage.
Contagious: No
Affected Population: Adults, especially overweight or with family history.
Type: Chronic
