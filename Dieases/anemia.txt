Name: Anemia (Iron Deficiency)
Symptoms: Fatigue, weakness, pale skin, dizziness, cold hands and feet.
Causes: Low iron intake, blood loss, poor absorption, pregnancy.
Prevention: Iron-rich diet, iron supplements if needed.
Treatment: Iron supplements, dietary changes, treating underlying cause.
Diagnosis: Blood tests (CBC, iron studies).
Complications: Heart problems, complications in pregnancy, developmental delays in children.
Contagious: No
Affected Population: Women, children, people with poor diets.
Type: Chronic
