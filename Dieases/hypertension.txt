Name: Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)
Symptoms: Headaches, dizziness, blurred vision, often asymptomatic.
Causes: Stress, high salt intake, obesity, smoking, genetics.
Prevention: Low-sodium diet, stress management, regular exercise, avoiding tobacco.
Treatment: ACE inhibitors, beta blockers, diuretics, lifestyle changes.
Diagnosis: Regular blood pressure measurements.
Complications: Heart attack, stroke, kidney damage.
Contagious: No
Affected Population: Adults, especially over 40 years old.
Type: Chronic
