Name: Asthma
Symptoms: Wheezing, shortness of breath, chest tightness, coughing.
Causes: Allergies, pollution, respiratory infections, exercise triggers.
Prevention: Avoid allergens, manage triggers, use preventive inhalers.
Treatment: Inhaled corticosteroids, bronchodilators.
Diagnosis: Spirometry test, symptom assessment.
Complications: Frequent hospitalizations, reduced lung function.
Contagious: No
Affected Population: Children and adults with allergies or family history.
Type: Chronic
